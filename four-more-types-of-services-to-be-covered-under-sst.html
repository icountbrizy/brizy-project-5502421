Four more types of services to be covered under SST

Customs director-general Datuk Seri T. Subromaniam said the services thatll be taxed are amusement park operations, securities brokerage and underwriting, cleaning services, and training provision.

Companies providing such taxable services with total value of more than RM50,000 per annum must be registered with Customs on or before 28thFebruary 2019.

They have been given two months to register their business and prepare to implement service tax collection, he told the press conference.

Source: The Star Online
