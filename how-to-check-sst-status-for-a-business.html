How to check SST status for a business

Are you still worried about SST status for your business? Here are the simple 3 steps that you can refer to. Action now.

Step 1 :

Log in to: https://www.mysst.customs.gov.my/ and click on “Registration Status”.

Step 2 :

Select your choice of company information to be filled in.

Step 3 :

Fill in your company information and click submit. The result will be popping up right after your submission!
