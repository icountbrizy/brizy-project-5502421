LHDN: Statement form will not be printed anymore

Lembaga Hasil Dalam Negeri (LHDN) will not be priniting and posting statement form this yearr, as an initiative to encourage taxpayer to use the e-filing system.

If employers or tasxpayers that are not company or tax agent still need to submit statement form papers, they will have to download and print it themselves from the official website of LHDN. www.hasil.gov.my > menu Borang > Muat Turun Borang
